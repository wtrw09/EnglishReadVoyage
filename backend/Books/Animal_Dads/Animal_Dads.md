# World's Greatest DAD

Other animal dads are great! 

Which animal dads are those? 

![image](./assets/image_05.jpg)


This dad guards and cares for his chicks. 

He helps them all by himself for almost two years. 

![image](./assets/image_01.jpg)


This dad cleans bugs from his babies' fur. 

He helps feed them. 

He carries them, too. 

![image](./assets/image_08.jpg)

He helps them stay safe until they are born.

and spits it back up.
